# Lost Mobile Device Incident

## Scenario
A user reported a lost company mobile phone through a Priority 1 ticket.

## Identification
I reviewed the ticket, confirmed the device was lost, and treated it as a potential security incident because the phone could contain corporate access or data.

## Containment
I created a request for the appropriate team to remotely wipe the phone. I also created a separate request to remove or disable the cellular service.

## Eradication
The security risk was reduced by removing access to corporate data and disabling the device’s service.

## Recovery
I provided the user with the correct process to request a replacement phone.

## Lessons Learned
Lost devices should be handled quickly because they can expose corporate data, authentication tokens, email, or MFA access.

## Interview Notes
This example shows incident response, risk reduction, escalation, asset protection, and business continuity.
